Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2017

Information Collection and Use

This Service requires you to give read-only permissions to fetch Calendar Resources and upcoming events from Calendar Resource Calendars.

This service does not share data received from Calendar Resources or Calendars with third parties.

Calendar Resources or Calendars will be cached by your browser but not received/stored by this Service's servers.

Calendar Resources Usage

This Service requires read-only access to your G Suite calendar resources. You can find your calendar resources at https://admin.google.com/ac/calendarresources/resources. This Service will fetch and display the calendar resources (for example a conference room) during the initial setup process. During the setup process you assign a calendar resource to the meeting room TV.

Calendar Usage

This Service requires read-only access to your G Suite calendars. G Suite creates a calendar for each calendar resource, and this service will read from those calendars only. After the initial setup, this Service continuously fetches the upcoming events from calendar resource calendars to display them on the meeting room TV.
